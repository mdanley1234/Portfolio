# Urban Scooter Project

The Urban Scooter Project (USP) is a proof-of-concept prototype to test the viability of ultra-portable lightweight scooter design using innovative mechanisms and non-traditional materials.

## Main Frame Design

* **Deck:** Three-part 1/8" 6061 aluminum deck plate, target mass ~207 g.
* **Structure:** Carbon fiber tubing for the primary structural members — high strength-to-weight ratio and low mass (single tube 200 g).
* **Bracing:** Custom aluminum braces connect plate sections to carbon fiber tubes. Iterated via FEA to minimize weight (~22 g per brace) while maintaining a safety factor ≈ 4 at 1000 N static load.

## Collapsible Mechanism

Tubes of varying diameters slide on bushings enabling >30% collapse in length for compact storage. Design focuses on lightweight, durable locking geometry.

## Full Mechanical Assembly

Assembly includes the deck, telescoping front steering assembly, and wheels. Parts are either off-the-shelf or custom-fabricated. The project is currently funded and in fabrication — renders of the full and folded assemblies are included.
